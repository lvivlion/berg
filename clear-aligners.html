Clarity™ Clear Aligners

A Discreet & Effective Way to Straighten Your Smile

At Pittsburgh Clear Aligner Center, we're proud to offer Clarity™ Clear Aligners—a comfortable, nearly invisible way to achieve a beautifully aligned smile. These advanced, custom-made aligners provide an excellent alternative to traditional braces, allowing you to straighten your teeth with confidence.

Why Choose Clarity™ Clear Aligners?

  • Virtually Invisible

    Made from a transparent, smooth material—perfect for patients who want a discreet orthodontic solution.

  • Comfortable & Removable

    Unlike braces, these aligners are removable for eating, brushing, and flossing. Smooth edges ensure comfort.

  • Customized for You

    Each set is custom-designed using advanced digital technology for precise tooth movement.

  • Effective for a Variety of Cases

    From minor adjustments to comprehensive treatment—addresses a wide range of alignment and bite issues.

How Do Clarity™ Clear Aligners Work?

1

Consultation

Detailed consultation with 3D digital imaging to map your treatment plan.

2

Custom Aligners

Your aligners are designed to gradually shift teeth into ideal position.

3

Wear & Progress

Each set worn for 1-2 weeks with gradual adjustments along the way.

4

New Smile!

Receive retainers to maintain your perfect, beautiful smile.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Clarity™ Clear Aligners are ideal for teens and adults looking for a discreet way to straighten their teeth. They work well for:

Mild to moderate crowding
Gaps between teeth
Overbites & underbites
Alternative to braces

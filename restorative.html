Restorative Services

Restoring your smile's function and aesthetics.

Composite Fillings

White fillings that can be easily matched to the natural shade of your tooth. We do our best to prevent cavities, but in the case a patient develops one, prompt treatment helps prevent further damage to the tooth.

Crown and Bridge Work

Crowns and bridges are options that help us to replace and repair your teeth, restoring them to full functionality and aesthetic appearance. Made from a porcelain material, crowns and bridges are placed over the natural tooth to provide support and maintain a natural appearance.

Implant Crowns

A dental implant is an ideal tooth restoration for people who are missing one or more teeth as a result of injury, periodontal disease, or any other reason. Once in place and bone surrounding the implant has had time to heal, a replacement tooth is attached to the post.

While implants are typically more expensive than other methods of tooth replacement, they provide superior benefits. Implants are stronger than natural teeth and generally last 10-20 years. They are also a more favorable approach than bridgework since they do not depend on neighboring teeth for support.

To receive implants, you need to have healthy gums and adequate bone to support the implant. You must also be committed to excellent oral hygiene and regular dental visits as these are critical to the long-term success of dental implants.

Root Canals and Extractions

We perform root canal therapy and extractions on select teeth which can be determined at the time of your appointment.

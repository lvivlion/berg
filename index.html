Welcome to Berg Dental

At Berg Dental, we are dedicated to providing exceptional dental care that prioritizes your comfort, health, and a beautiful smile. Established in 2002 by Dr. Berg, our practice combines a warm, welcoming environment with the latest advancements in dentistry. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients, ensuring every visit is a positive and informed experience.

As the Pittsburgh Clear Aligner Center, we specialize in state-of-the-art clear aligner technology, offering discreet and effective orthodontic solutions for both adults and children. Achieve the confident smile you've always dreamed of with our custom-crafted treatment plans.

Our commitment is to guide you through every procedure with clear explanations and personalized care, ensuring your decisions are always for your optimal dental well-being.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Dental Services

Restorative Dentistry

From durable composite fillings to precision crowns and bridges, and implant crowns, we restore the function and aesthetics of your smile with advanced techniques like root canals.

BioLase Water Laser

Experience a more comfortable and precise dental procedure with our Waterlase technology, utilizing laser energy and water spray, often without the need for shots or drills.

3Shape Trios Scanner

Say goodbye to messy, uncomfortable impressions. Our 3Shape Trios Scanner provides quick, accurate, and digital impressions for a superior patient experience.

Preventative Care

Maintaining excellent oral hygiene is fundamental. We offer comprehensive preventative care to keep your teeth and gums healthy and prevent future dental issues.
